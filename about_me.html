With the band, I have actively toured and played to sold out shows in London and Manchester and amassed 227k combined streams on Spotify by releasing self-produced original singles. The band has been an amazing outlet for my creative growth and it also enabled me to support and devote my passion to various charitable causes close to my heart such as Oxjam and Teenage Cancer Trust.

I have also trained for 11 years as a classical guitarist in Rome and won major Italian and European guitar competitions before realising that studying Mathematics was what I really wanted to do with my life and started my University experience at UCL (London).

I’m a keen sportsperson, having played and trained in football, volleyball, swimming, tennis and table-tennis. In my spare time you can find me playing chess or binging the latest Netflix series.
