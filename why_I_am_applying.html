I am very excited to be applying for the Founders and Coders programme and to submit this website for your review. I first encountered coding while studying Mathematics at University and started using Wolfram Mathematica in order to obtain solutions to complex equations and to geometrically visualise such solutions for further analysis. I quickly realised its potential and decided to study in more depth Mathematica and Python by attending summer courses offered by the University. When I completed my studies, I started working as a finance assistant at Fluidly, a fast growing FinTech start-up in London, where I was introduced to the exciting world of tech and exposed to the frenetic and tireless life of a start-up. In particular, the bewildering work being done around the desks of the engineering team caught my attention and sparked my interest in web development.
